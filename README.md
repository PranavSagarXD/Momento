**Momento** is a privacy-first, offline Android app for capturing, organizing, and reliving your daily moments. Turn your photos into beautiful montage videos with customizable transitions, face stabilization, and more — all on your device, no data ever leaves.

## Features

- [x] Montage maker with customizable transitions and effects
- [x] Auto face stabilization for smooth video output
- [x] Calendar view to browse memories by date
- [x] Daily reminders to keep your moment journal going
- [x] Offline-first — everything stays on your device
- [x] Material You theming with dynamic colors
- [x] Open source and privacy friendly

## Motivation

I wanted a simple, elegant app to effortlessly create daily montage videos like [this one](https://youtu.be/65nfbW-27ps). The idea had been brewing for over a month, and building Momento became the perfect challenge. Every feature is designed to be offline-first and privacy-respecting — your memories never leave your device.
